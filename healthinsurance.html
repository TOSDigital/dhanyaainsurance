General Insurance

General insurance is a type of insurance that protects individuals and businesses from financial losses caused by unexpected events, such as accidents, natural disasters, and theft. It covers a wide range of insurance policies, including car insurance, home insurance, and business insurance, among others.

Life Insurance

Life insurance is a contract between an insurer and a policyholder in which the insurer guarantees payment of a death benefit to named beneficiaries upon the death of the insured. It is designed to provide financial protection for the policyholder's loved ones in the event of their death. There are various types of life insurance, including term life insurance, whole life insurance, and universal life insurance, among others.

Top Up Plan

We understand the importance of having access to quality healthcare and the financial burden of unexpected medical expenses.That's why we offer a Top Up Plan, which is designed to provide additional coverage over and above the limit of the basic health insurance policy.Our Top Up Plan typically has a higher coverage limit compared to the base policy and it kicks in when the expenses incurred exceed a certain threshold, known as a deductible. This threshold is usually the sum insured of the base policy. Once the threshold is crossed, the Top Up Plan starts covering the expenses.Top Up Plan is that it can be customised to suit individual needs and budget. It is an affordable way to enhance the coverage of the basic health insurance policy without having to pay for a higher premium. This type of plan is suitable for people who are looking for additional protection against high medical expenses, without having to pay for a higher premium for a full-fledged health insurance policy.

Critical illness

Critical Illness insurance is a type of insurance coverage that provides a lump sum benefit to the insured upon the diagnosis of a critical illness. These illnesses can include conditions such as cancer, heart attack, stroke, kidney failure, and major organ transplants, among others. The coverage and the list of illnesses covered may vary based on the insurance company and the policy selected. This type of insurance is designed to provide financial assistance for those who are diagnosed with a serious illness. It can help to cover the cost of medical treatment, including surgery and hospitalisation, as well as other expenses such as loss of income, transportation, and accommodation. At Dhanya Insurance, we offer Critical Illness insurance coverage to protect you and your loved ones from the financial burden of unexpected medical expenses. Our experienced team can work with you to find the right coverage and limits to fit your specific needs and budget.

Hospital Cash

Hospital Cash insurance is a type of insurance coverage that provides a daily cash benefit to the insured during hospitalisation. The benefit is paid directly to the policyholder and can be used to cover expenses such as transportation, accommodation, or other out-of-pocket expenses that may be incurred during the hospital stay.It helps to alleviate the financial burden of unexpected medical expenses, allowing the policyholder to focus on their recovery. The coverage and the amount of cash benefit may vary based on the insurance company and the policy selected.One of the benefits of Hospital Cash insurance is that it pays the benefit directly to the insured, regardless of other insurance coverage they may have. This allows the policyholder to use the benefit in any way they see fit to help cover the expenses associated with their hospital stay.

Cancer 360 Degree

Cancer 360 Degree is a comprehensive cancer insurance coverage that provides financial assistance for individuals who are diagnosed with cancer. It covers the cost of cancer treatment, including surgery, chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and other medical expenses. This type of coverage is designed to provide support for both the policyholder and their family during a difficult time. The coverage and the benefits of Cancer 360 Degree insurance may vary based on the insurance company and the policy selected. Some policies may cover pre-existing conditions, while others may have waiting periods before coverage begins. The policyholder may also have the option to add additional riders for more comprehensive coverage. One of the benefits of Cancer 360 Degree insurance is that it provides a lump sum benefit upon diagnosis of cancer. This allows the policyholder to use the benefit in any way they see fit to help cover the expenses associated with their cancer treatment.
